<p><ul><li>Fiber Yarn</li><li>Fiber Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberglass yarn market includes two primary types: fiber yarn and fiber roving. Fiber yarn consists of continuous strands of fiberglass, offering high strength and flexibility, suitable for weaving into fabrics or reinforcing composites. In contrast, fiber roving comprises loose strands or bundles of fiberglass that can be easily manipulated, making it ideal for applications requiring bulk reinforcement, such as in construction and automotive industries. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing durability and performance in various composite materials.</p></p>
