<p><p>Medium-voltage Gas-insulated Switchgear (GIS) is a compact and efficient technology used in electrical distribution systems, typically operating in the voltage range of 1 kV to 38 kV. GIS utilizes gas (usually sulfur hexafluoride) as an insulation medium, allowing for a significant reduction in required space compared to traditional air-insulated switchgear. This technology is particularly advantageous in urban areas where space is limited and in applications requiring high reliability and low maintenance.</p><p>The Medium-voltage Gas-insulated Switchgear Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.6% during the forecast period, driven by several factors.
