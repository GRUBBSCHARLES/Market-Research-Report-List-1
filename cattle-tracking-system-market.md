<p><p>Cattle Tracking Systems are categorized into three main market types: GPS-based, radio-based, and other technologies. GPS-based systems utilize satellite signals for real-time location tracking, offering high accuracy and wide coverage. Radio-based systems rely on short-range signals, suitable for confined areas but providing limited range. Other technologies may include Bluetooth or RFID, which facilitate tracking through close-range communication. Each type addresses specific needs in cattle management, ensuring efficient monitoring, enhanced security, and improved herd management practices.</p></p>
