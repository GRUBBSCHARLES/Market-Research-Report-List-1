<p><p>The breast-shaped tissue expanders market is segmented into two primary types: dual-port and one-port expanders. Dual-port expanders feature two access points for fluid injection, allowing for more precise adjustment of the expander volume, which can enhance patient comfort and optimize breast contouring. In contrast, one-port expanders have a single access point, simplifying the expansion process but offering less flexibility in volume adjustments. Both types serve the critical function of preparing the breast for permanent reconstruction after mastectomy.</p></p>
