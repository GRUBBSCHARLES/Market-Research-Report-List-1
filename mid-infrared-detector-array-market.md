<p><p>The mid-infrared detector array market encompasses various types, including Indium Antimonide (InSb), Lead Selenide (PbSe), and Lead Sulfide (PbS). InSb detectors are known for their high sensitivity and rapid response times, making them ideal for applications in thermal imaging and spectroscopy. PbSe detectors offer good performance in the short-wave infrared range, commonly used in gas detection and chemical analysis. PbS detectors, while less sensitive, are cost-effective for applications like environmental monitoring and basic imaging systems. Each type serves distinct applications within the broader mid-infrared spectrum.</p></p>
