<p><p>The bread improvers market consists of various types, including emulsifiers, enzymes, oxidizing agents, and reducing agents. Emulsifiers enhance dough stability and texture, improving moisture retention. Enzymes, such as amylases, break down starches to enhance fermentation and dough performance. Oxidizing agents strengthen gluten structure, promoting volume and crumb texture, while reducing agents counteract oxidation, helping to maintain softness. These components collectively optimize bread quality, extending shelf life and improving overall consumer appeal in the baked goods industry.</p></p>
