<p><p>The thoracic surgery market encompasses various specialized procedures targeting cardiovascular issues. Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG) involves rerouting blood around blocked arteries to improve heart function. Heart Valve Repair addresses valve dysfunctions, restoring proper blood flow. Transcatheter Heart Valve Replacement (TAVR) offers a minimally invasive approach to replace faulty heart valves. Left Atrial Appendage (LAA) Closure prevents stroke by sealing off this heart chamber. Thoracic Endovascular Aortic Repair (TEVAR) treats aortic aneurysms using stent grafts, enhancing patient outcomes in complex cardiac conditions.</p></p>
