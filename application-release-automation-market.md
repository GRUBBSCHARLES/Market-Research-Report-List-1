<p><p>Application Release Automation (ARA) market can be categorized into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises ARA installs software directly on a company’s servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance and management. In contrast, cloud-based ARA solutions are hosted on external servers, providing scalability, faster deployment, and lower upfront costs. These cloud solutions facilitate collaboration and access from anywhere, appealing to businesses seeking efficiency and reduced infrastructure burden in their software delivery processes.</p></p>
