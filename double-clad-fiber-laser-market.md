<p><p>The Double-Clad Fiber Laser market is primarily segmented into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Lasers and Pulsed Fiber Lasers. CW Fiber Lasers produce a constant output, ideal for applications requiring continuous operation, such as cutting and welding. In contrast, Pulsed Fiber Lasers emit energy in short bursts, providing high peak power for processes like marking and engraving. Both types leverage the double-clad fiber design for enhanced efficiency and performance, catering to diverse industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
