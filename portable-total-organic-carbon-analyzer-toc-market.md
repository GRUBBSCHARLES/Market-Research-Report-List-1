<p><p>The Portable Total Organic Carbon (TOC) Analyzer market includes three main types: Solid TOC Analyzers, Liquid TOC Analyzers, and Solid-Liquid Integrated Analyzers. Solid TOC Analyzers are designed to measure carbon content in solid samples, making them ideal for soil and sediment analysis. Liquid TOC Analyzers focus on assessing organic carbon levels in liquids, useful for water quality testing. Solid-Liquid Integrated Analyzers combine the functionalities of both types, providing versatility for various environmental and industrial applications, enhancing efficiency in TOC measurement.</p></p>
