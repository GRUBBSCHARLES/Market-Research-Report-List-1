<p><ul><li>Two Pipe FCU Valve</li><li>Four Pipe FCU Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fan Coil Unit (FCU) valve market is divided into two main types: Two Pipe FCU valves and Four Pipe FCU valves. Two Pipe FCU valves operate using a single set of pipes for both heating and cooling, switching between the two based on the season. In contrast, Four Pipe FCU valves have separate pipes for heating and cooling, allowing simultaneous temperature control and enhanced comfort. This distinction affects system design, efficiency, and application in various building types.</p></p>
