<p><strong>The Die Cutting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal-to-Metal</li><li>Perfing</li><li>Slitting</li><li>Kiss Cutting</li><li>Scoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The die cutting machine market encompasses various techniques tailored for specific applications. Metal-to-metal die cutting involves precise cutting of materials, typically in manufacturing. Perfing creates perforations, allowing for easy tearing along designated lines. Slitting refers to cutting materials into strips, often used in packaging. Kiss cutting provides a clean cut through the top layer while leaving the backing intact, ideal for stickers. Scoring creates indentation lines, enabling easier folding, commonly used in box manufacturing and paper products.</p></p>
