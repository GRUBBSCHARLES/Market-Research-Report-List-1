<p><p>The Converged Data Center Infrastructure market consists of two primary types: Reference Architecture and Pre-Racked Configuration. Reference Architecture provides a standardized framework that integrates various hardware and software components to streamline data center deployment and management. It guides organizations in building efficient, scalable solutions. Pre-Racked Configuration, on the other hand, involves a ready-to-install system where components are pre-assembled in a rack, enabling quicker deployment and reducing setup complexity. Both types enhance operational efficiency and simplify data center management.</p></p>
