<p><p>PCD (Pharmaceutical Contract Distribution) markets consist of two main types: PDS (Pharmaceutical Distribution Services) and PDC (Pharmaceutical Distribution Centers). PDSs provide specialized distribution services to manage the supply chain, ensuring efficient delivery of pharmaceutical products to healthcare providers. On the other hand, PDCs serve as centralized facilities that handle storage and distribution logistics, enabling streamlined operations. Both types focus on optimizing the distribution process, ensuring compliance, and maintaining the integrity of pharmaceutical products throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
