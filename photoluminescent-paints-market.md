<p><p>Photoluminescent paints are categorized by their particle sizes, which influence their application and brightness. Coarse powder paints (above 25 µm) are used for outdoor applications due to their durability and visibility. Fine powder paints (3-5 µm) offer a balance of brightness and smooth finish, suitable for various surfaces. Ground fine powder paints (1-3 µm) provide the highest luminosity and the finest texture, making them ideal for detailed artistic works and specific effects in indoor settings. Each type serves unique market needs based on performance and aesthetic requirements.</p></p>
