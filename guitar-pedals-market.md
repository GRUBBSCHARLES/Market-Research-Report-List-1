<p><p>The guitar pedal market consists of various types tailored to enhance sound. Gain effects, like overdrive and distortion, amplify signal strength and add character. Compressors control dynamics for a smoother output. Pitch effects, including harmonizers and pitch shifters, modify the pitch of notes. Volume pedals manage sound intensity. Filter effects shape frequency response, creating unique tones. Time-based effects, such as delays and reverbs, manipulate sound duration and space. Lastly, other pedals encompass specialty effects, like noise gates and loopers, catering to diverse musical needs.</p></p>
