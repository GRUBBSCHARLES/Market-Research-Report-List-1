<p><p>The urethane coating additive market is primarily divided into two types: one-component and two-component polyurethane coatings. One-component polyurethane coatings are ready-to-use products that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient for various applications. In contrast, two-component polyurethane coatings require mixing a resin with a hardener before application, offering superior durability and chemical resistance. These coatings are commonly used in industrial, automotive, and architectural applications due to their exceptional performance and versatility.</p></p>
