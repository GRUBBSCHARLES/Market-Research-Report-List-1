<p><strong>The Nitinol based Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent IVC Filters</li><li>Retrievable IVC Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nitinol-based filters market primarily comprises two types: Permanent IVC filters and Retrievable IVC filters. Permanent IVC filters are designed for long-term implantation to prevent pulmonary embolism by trapping blood clots, remaining in the body indefinitely. In contrast, Retrievable IVC filters provide temporary protection, allowing for removal after the risk period has passed, thus minimizing complications and enhancing patient comfort. Both types leverage Nitinol's unique properties, offering flexibility, biocompatibility, and resistance to corrosion, crucial for vascular applications.</p></p>
