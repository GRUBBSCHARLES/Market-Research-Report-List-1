<p><p>The dental flowable composite materials market is divided into two primary types: opaque and translucent composites. Opaque composites are designed to provide excellent coverage and conceal underlying tooth structures, making them ideal for addressing discoloration and achieving aesthetic results in restorative procedures. In contrast, translucent composites mimic the natural appearance of tooth enamel, allowing them to blend seamlessly with surrounding teeth. Both types cater to varying clinical needs, ensuring dental professionals can achieve optimal aesthetics and functionality in restorations.</p></p>
