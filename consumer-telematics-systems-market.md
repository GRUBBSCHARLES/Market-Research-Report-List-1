<p><strong>The Consumer Telematics Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated Telematics</li><li>Embedded Telematics</li><li>Tethered Telematics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Consumer telematics systems encompass three main types: integrated, embedded, and tethered telematics. Integrated telematics combines hardware and software directly within the vehicle, providing seamless functionality and connectivity. Embedded telematics features built-in systems that operate independently, offering services like navigation and diagnostics without reliance on external devices. Tethered telematics relies on external devices, such as smartphones, to connect with the vehicle, allowing users to access telematics services through apps and cloud platforms, enhancing convenience and functionality.</p></p>
