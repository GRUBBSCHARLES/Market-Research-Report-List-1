<p><p>Surge Protection Devices (SPDs) are essential for safeguarding electrical systems from voltage spikes caused by lightning or equipment switching. The Voltage Switch Type SPD activates when voltage exceeds a set threshold, diverting excess energy. Pressure Limiting Type SPD, on the other hand, employs pressure to manage surge levels, ensuring consistent operation under high-stress conditions. Combination Type SPD blends features of both, offering comprehensive protection by addressing both voltage spikes and pressure variations, making them versatile for diverse applications.</p></p>
