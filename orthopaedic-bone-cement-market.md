<p><p>The orthopaedic bone cement market is categorized into low, medium, and high viscosity cements, each suited for specific applications. Low viscosity cements are used for deep bone penetration and optimal flow in minimally invasive procedures. Medium viscosity cements balance ease of handling and optimal fixation. High viscosity cements offer excellent mechanical strength and resistance to leakage, making them suitable for large bone reconstructive surgeries. These diverse types cater to varying surgical needs and enhance patient outcomes in orthopaedic interventions.</p></p>
