<p><p>MRO (Maintenance, Repair, and Operations) distribution in the footwear market encompasses two primary segments: preventive/scheduled maintenance and corrective maintenance. Preventive maintenance involves regularly scheduled upkeep to extend the lifespan of machinery and equipment used in footwear production, ensuring optimal performance and reducing downtime. In contrast, corrective maintenance addresses repairs after equipment failures occur, restoring operations to standard. Both types are crucial in maintaining efficiency, reducing costs, and enhancing productivity in the highly competitive footwear industry.</p></p>
