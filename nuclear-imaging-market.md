<p><strong>The Nuclear Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positron Emission Tomography</li><li>Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography</li><li>Planar Scintigraphy Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear imaging market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Positron Emission Tomography (PET)** utilizes radioactive tracers to visualize metabolic processes, aiding in cancer detection and treatment efficacy assessments. </p><p>2. **Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT)** captures 3D images of tracer distribution, valuable for cardiac, brain, and bone imaging. </p><p>3. **Planar Scintigraphy Systems** offer 2D images, suited for various diagnostic applications, like evaluating organ function and detecting abnormalities. These technologies enhance diagnostic precision through functional imaging.</p></p>
