<p><p>The Airplane Solenoid Valves market includes two primary types: 2-way and 3-way solenoid valves. 2-way solenoid valves control the flow of fluids in a single direction, crucial for applications needing simple on/off control, making them ideal for fuel systems and hydraulic operations. In contrast, 3-way solenoid valves manage fluid flow in multiple directions, allowing for more complex routing and control, often used in applications that require mixing or diverting fluids, enhancing operational flexibility in aerospace environments.</p></p>
