<p><p>The brain implants market includes three primary types: Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS), Spinal Cord Stimulation (SCS), and Vagus Nerve Stimulation (VNS). DBS involves implanting electrodes in specific brain areas to treat movement disorders and psychiatric conditions. SCS delivers electrical impulses to the spinal cord to alleviate chronic pain. VNS involves stimulating the vagus nerve to manage epilepsy and depression. These technologies are gaining traction due to their effectiveness in treating neurological disorders and improving patients' quality of life.</p></p>
