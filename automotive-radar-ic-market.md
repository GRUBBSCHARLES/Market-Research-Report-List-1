The 24 GHz radar is typically utilized for short-range applications like parking assistance and obstacle detection, providing effective performance in close proximity. Conversely, the 77 GHz radar is preferred for long-range applications such as adaptive cruise control and collision avoidance, offering higher resolution and better object differentiation. Both types are essential for enhancing vehicle safety and enabling advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS).</p></p>
