<p><p>The IPTV Set-Top Box market comprises various types, including OTT (Over-the-Top) and DTT (Direct Terrestrial Transmission). OTT services deliver content directly over the internet, bypassing traditional cable or satellite systems, offering convenience and flexibility to users. In contrast, DTT utilizes terrestrial signals to transmit broadcasting content directly to set-top boxes, requiring an antenna for reception. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences, influencing their adoption and growth within the digital entertainment landscape.</p></p>
