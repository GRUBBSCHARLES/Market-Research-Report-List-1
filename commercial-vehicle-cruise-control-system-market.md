<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Cruise Control System Market is divided into two main types: Semi-Automatic Control and Full Automatic Control. Semi-Automatic Control allows drivers to maintain a set speed while still retaining the ability to manually adjust acceleration and braking. In contrast, Full Automatic Control takes over these tasks completely, enabling the vehicle to autonomously adjust speed based on traffic, road conditions, and other factors. Both systems enhance driving comfort and fuel efficiency while improving overall safety in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
