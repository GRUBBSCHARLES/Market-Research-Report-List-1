<p><p>The COPD medication market is diverse, encompassing several types of treatments. Short-acting bronchodilators provide quick relief by opening airways, while long-acting bronchodilators offer extended control of symptoms. Corticosteroids reduce inflammation in the airways, improving lung function. Methylxanthines act as bronchodilators but are less commonly used. Combination drugs leverage the benefits of multiple medications, enhancing overall effectiveness and adherence in managing COPD. This variety caters to different patient needs, driving growth in the COPD treatment landscape.</p></p>
