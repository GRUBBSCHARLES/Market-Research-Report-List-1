<p><p>Absorbable dental barrier membranes are advanced materials used in dental procedures such as guided bone regeneration (GBR) and periodontal treatments. These membranes serve as physical barriers, promoting tissue regeneration while preventing the infiltration of non-osteogenic tissue. Made from biocompatible materials that gradually dissolve in the body, they eliminate the need for a second surgery to remove the barrier, thus enhancing patient comfort and recovery.</p><p>The Absorbable Dental Barrier Membrane Market is witnessing substantial growth, driven by an increasing prevalence of dental disorders, advancements in dental technology, and rising awareness of oral health.
