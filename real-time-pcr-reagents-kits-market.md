<p><ul><li>One-step Real Time PCR</li><li>Two-step Real Time PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Real-Time PCR Reagents & Kits market is primarily categorized into two types: One-step Real-Time PCR and Two-step Real-Time PCR. One-step Real-Time PCR involves a single tube where reverse transcription and PCR occur simultaneously, enhancing efficiency and reducing contamination risk. In contrast, Two-step Real-Time PCR separates the reverse transcription and amplification processes into two distinct steps, allowing for more flexibility and optimization of each reaction phase. Both types cater to different experimental needs and provide versatile options for researchers.</p></p>
