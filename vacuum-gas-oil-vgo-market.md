<p><p>Vacuum Gas Oil (VGO) is a key intermediate product derived from the vacuum distillation of crude oil, primarily used in producing diesel and other refined products. The VGO market is categorized into Heavy Vacuum Gas Oil (HVGO) and Light Vacuum Gas Oil (LVGO). HVGO, with higher viscosity and density, is often utilized for further processing in hydrocracking units. In contrast, LVGO is lighter, making it suitable for blending with lighter products and reducing the overall sulfur content in fuels.</p></p>
