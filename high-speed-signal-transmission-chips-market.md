<p><p>The high-speed signal transmission chips market includes various types tailored for specific functions. Switch chips manage the transmission and reception of signals, allowing for dynamic communication routes. Distribute chips focus on signal distribution across multiple outputs, enhancing connectivity. Matrix switch chips provide complex signal routing management, enabling multiple input-output configurations, ideal for applications requiring flexible signal handling in data centers and telecommunications.</p></p>
