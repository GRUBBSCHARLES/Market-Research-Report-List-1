<p><p>The Non-invasive Positive Pressure Ventilators market for in-home use is primarily divided into two types: Single Level Ventilators and Bilevel Ventilators. Single Level Ventilators deliver a constant level of air pressure throughout the breathing cycle, supporting patients with conditions like sleep apnea. In contrast, Bilevel Ventilators provide two distinct pressure levels—higher for inhalation and lower for exhalation—allowing for more comfortable and effective breathing support. Both types enhance patient quality of life while enabling at-home management of respiratory issues.</p></p>
