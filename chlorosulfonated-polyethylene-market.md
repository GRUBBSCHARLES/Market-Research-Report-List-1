<p><p>Chlorosulfonated Polyethylene (CSM) is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I is characterized by a higher degree of chlorosulfonation, resulting in enhanced chemical and thermal resistance, making it ideal for applications in harsh environments such as automotive and industrial sectors. Type II, on the other hand, has a lower chlorosulfonation level, offering better flexibility and processability, suitable for consumer goods and various adhesive applications.
