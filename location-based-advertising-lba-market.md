<p><p>Location-Based Advertising (LBA) leverages geographic information to deliver targeted marketing messages to consumers based on their real-time location. Various market types enhance location accuracy: Assisted GPS (A-GPS) combines satellite signals with network data for improved reliability; GPS uses satellite signals for precise positioning; Enhanced GPS (E-GPS) enhances accuracy using additional data sources. Enhanced Observed Time Difference (E-OTD) and Observed Time Difference (OTD) utilize cellular towers for location triangulation. Cell ID identifies users via cell towers, while Wi-Fi positioning uses nearby networks for location identification, along with other emerging technologies.</p></p>
